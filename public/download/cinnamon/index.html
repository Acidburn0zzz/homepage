Manjaro Cinnamon Edition


This edition is supported by the Manjaro community and comes with Cinnamon, a desktop based on modern technology that keeps known and proven concepts.

Download

You can choose between a 64bit version for modern computers and a 32bit version for older computers.

You can also use Torrents to download Manjaro. Just download the *.torrent file for the 64 bit or 32 bit version and open it with a torrent program like qBittorrent.

Be secure

We encourage you to check if the download was not modified by some malicious person or by an error while downloading. As a simple check, you can generate the SHA1-sum of the downloaded file and compare it with the one we provide you here. Here you can find information how to generate such a sum and check your downloaded files.

To even further enhance the security, verify the downloaded file with a GPG (GNU Privacy Guard) signature that will ensure that the file was not modified and was actually created by the Manjaro developers. Here you can find information how to use it.

Getting started

After downloading and checking the file, you can put it on a DVD or a USB drive. You will find step-by-step guides in the Manjaro Wiki.
